Gameplay

Each player has to pass out one card facing down in their turn. The player on the left has to decide whether accept this card or pass it to the next player.

Beginning Session: The first session of a new round. The player of this round will have a drawing session right after the beginning session.

Drawing Session: Drawing session appears after the Beginning session. The Player could draw 2 cards from the deck.

Action Session: The session right after the drawing session. The player of this round can uses any function cards or skills which are allowed to be used during action session.

Mailing Session: The player of this round should pass a mailing card facing down to the player on his left. If the next player does not accept the mailing card, he/she should pass it to the next player. The mailing card should be passed in clock order. Those who decide to accept this card should do a sanity test with it. If no other player accept this mailing card, the player of this round should do a sanity test with this card.

    Sanity

    Once the player accepts the mailing card, he/she should reveal this card. If it‚Äôs a red card(vision), the player will lose 1 sanity. A player lost all his/her sanity must quit the game immediately.

    Sanity Test: Reveal the mailing card. If it is knowledge, the player should add this card into his/her data folder. If it is vision, the player will lose 1 sanity. Those who accept the mailing card must do the sanity test.

      Knowledge

      During the sanity test, if the mailing card turns out to be black(knowledge), the player who accepts it should place it in his/her data folder. The player who first collect 6 knowledge cards can claim his/her victory.

                                                        Terms

                                                        A - Z

                                                        • Action Session: The session right after the drawing session. The player of this round can uses any function cards or skills which are allowed to be used during action session.

                                                        • Beginning Session: The first session of a new round. The player of this round will have a drawing session right after the beginning session.

                                                        • Character Card: The card represents the hero player used during the game. Before the game started, every player should draw a random investigator card. If the player is not satisfying with this card, he/she will have one more chance to draw a new investigator card randomly. Each player should not reveal their investigator card until the game started.

                                                        • Deck: The card deck made up with function cards. Deck will be placed in the center of the table. The drawing session will draw 2 cards from this deck. Some function cards such as Blood Moon and skills will also draw cards from this deck. If the deck was drawn to zero, the cards in the trash piles should be shuffled and turns into a new deck.

                                                        • Discard: Put a card into trash piles.

                                                        • Drawing Session: Drawing session appears after the Beginning session. The Player could draw 2 cards from the deck.

                                                        • Ending Session: The session after the the mailing session. The player of the round will end his/her round after ending session. The player on the left should begin a new round.

                                                        • Hand: The function cards in each player‚Äôs hand.

                                                        • Insane: A investigator loses his/her all sanity and leave the game immediately.

                                                        • Mailing Session: The player of this round should pass a mailing card facing down to the player on his left. If the next player does not accept the mailing card, he/she should pass it to the next player. The mailing card should be passed in clock order. Those who decide to accept this card should do a sanity test with it. If no other player accept this mailing card, the player of this round should do a sanity test with this card.

                                                        • Mailing Card: The card being passed during the Mailing Session.

                                                        • Sanity: Each investigator has a maximum sanity varies from 2 to more. When a player lost his/her all sanity, this player is considered insane and should leave the game. When a player trying to recover sanity, his/her current sanity cannot exceed the maximum sanity.

                                                        • Sanity Test: Reveal the mailing card. If it is knowledge, the player should add this card into his/her data folder. If it is vision, the player will lose 1 sanity. Those who accept the mailing card must do the sanity test.

                                                        • Trash Piles: Trash Piles should be placed in the center of the table. All the cards being discard would be placed in trash piles.
